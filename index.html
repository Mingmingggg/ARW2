What is ARW?

Annual Recruitment Week (ARW) is one of the most awaited annual activities in De La Salle University. Students are given the opportunity to choose among the 49 organizations under the Council of Student Organizations in pursuit of being able to meet people of similar interests and hone their skills outside of academic responsibilities.

×

ARW 2024

Annual Recruitment Week 2024: The Archer Calls welcomes each and every Lasallian tribute to an arena where each organization emerges as a district, showcasing their unique strengths and purpose. As the games begin, be there to witness what each organization has to offer. Join us to explore, engage, and discover how you can be part of something extraordinary. The games are on, and the choice is yours—find your path, align with your passion, and make your mark in the arena of possibilities.
